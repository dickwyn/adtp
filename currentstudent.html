Social

For current students, Sunway ADTP has a dedicated Facebook group which is a platform for students and the faculy to easily share a message and also acts as a notice board for forthcoming projects/events.

Sunway ADTP Group

Students which have already transferred or are awaiting for the transfer can join our LinkedIn Alumni group which serves as a platform to re-connect with ex-ADTP students to gain better expeosure towards forming a career after graduation.
