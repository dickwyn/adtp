Liberal Arts
Learn more »
Future students wishing to continue their studeis in the field of Business Administration, Accounting, Marketing, etc and will be exposed to subjets like social sciences, humanities, and arts. Includes majors like Business, Communications, Liberal Arts, etc.

Admission Requirements

Intakes in January, March, June and August
SPM / O-Level: Pass with minimum 5 credits inclusive of English.
UEC: Pass with minimum 5 Grade 'B' inclusive of English.
Any other qualification will be considered on a case-to-case basis.
English Language Requirements: IEP - Level 4; Cambridge English (First) - 50 and above
Science/Engineering
Learn more »
Future students who wish to pursue a field of study in Sciences or Engineering will learn to utilize concept and theories from famous scientists to prove and explore the possiblities. Science/Engineering majors will be covering topics from calculus, physics, chemistry and computer science. Includes any science related majors like Engineering, Aviation, Computer Science, etc.

Admission Requirements

Intakes in January, March, June and August
SPM / O-Level: Pass with minimum 5 credits inclusive of English and Mathematics or any Sciences.
UEC: Pass with minimum 5 Grade 'B' inclusive of English & Mathematics or any Science subject.
Any other qualification will be considered on a case-to-case basis.
English Language Requirements: IEP - Level 4; Cambridge English (First) - 50 and above
